Frequently Asked

Can I get a refund if it wasn't useful?

Yes. If after the call you feel I didn't add value, email me within 7 days. Full refund, no questions asked. I'd rather have an empty calendar than an unhappy person.

Is this SEBI-registered investment advice?

No, and I want to be honest about this. I'm not a SEBI Registered Investment Advisor (RIA). I cannot recommend specific stocks, mutual funds, or investment products. I CAN explain how things work, share frameworks for thinking, review your existing financial picture, and give general guidance. For specific investment recommendations, consult a SEBI RIA.

What if I'm not in India?

That's fine — sessions happen on Zoom/WhatsApp video. Pay via PayPal or international card. Email me to set it up. I have NRI clients who do quarterly check-ins about their India investments and family loans.

Will you sell my contact to anyone?

Never. Your details are used for the call, follow-up, and (if you opt in) my newsletter. They are not shared with anyone, ever. I run this myself — there's no marketing team, no CRM company, no third party.

Why is the price so low?

Because I'm starting out. These prices will go up significantly once my schedule fills. The goal in Year 1 is to help as many real people as possible while building my consulting practice. Founder pricing applies until December 2026.

Can you teach me banking from scratch?

Yes — but better through the structured course (Banking Deep Dive, launching October 2026). 1-on-1 calls work best for specific situations or strategic decisions. Email me if you're unsure which to pick.
